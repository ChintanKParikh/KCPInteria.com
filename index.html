The KCP Story

Where passion meets precision, and dreams take shape

Every space has a story waiting to be told. At KCP INTERIA, we are storytellers, dream weavers, and space makers who believe that where you live, work, and gather should feel like the truest expression of who you are.

Since 2008, we have had the privilege of transforming over 150 spaces across Hyderabad and beyond. From family homes that echo with laughter to luxurious hotels that welcome travelers, from dynamic offices that spark creativity to chic commercial spaces that captivate customers. Each project is a journey we take together with our clients.

What sets us apart is the way we listen. We believe the best interiors emerge when creativity meets genuine understanding of how you live, what you love, and what makes your heart feel at home.

Founder & Visionary

With an unwavering focus on design and over a decade of hands-on experience, Chintan Parikh is the heart and soul of KCP INTERIA. What started as a dream to create beautiful spaces has blossomed into a journey of transforming lives through thoughtful design.

Known for boundless creativity, innovative thinking, and meticulous attention to detail, Chintan creates experiences through design. At the end of the day, it is about creating spaces where people love to live.

While guided by a strong creative vision, KCP INTERIA is powered by a close-knit team of designers and craftsmen who bring this vision to life through collaboration, precision, and shared values.

How We Work

Your journey from dream to reality, step by thoughtful step

1

Getting to Know You

We start with a warm conversation over coffee or chai. Tell us about your dreams, your daily rhythms, what makes you smile. We are all ears, because the best designs come from truly understanding your story.

2

Bringing Ideas to Life

Armed with your vision, we create initial concepts with sketches, boards, 3D visualizations, and material palettes. This is where magic starts to happen, where your wish becomes reality.

3

Refining Every Detail

We dive deep into the details. Every measurement, material, and light switch matters. Through realistic 3D renderings and detailed drawings, we refine until everything feels just right.

4

Making It Real

Our skilled team brings your design to life with careful craftsmanship and coordinated execution. We keep you updated every step of the way, because we know waiting for your dream space is exciting.

5

Welcome Home

The moment you have been waiting for. We hand over your completed space with final styling touches, complete documentation, and ongoing support.
